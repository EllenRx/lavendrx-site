The Problem

Clinical Data Is Abundant. Usable Data Is Not.

Hospitals and research centers generate enormous volumes of clinical data, but most of it is locked in unstructured formats that can't be used for research, quality improvement, or decision-making without significant manual effort.

Manual Chart Review Doesn't Scale

Trained abstractors spend 60+ minutes per routine case. Complex cases take five or six hours. A single registry program at a large health system requires over 11,000 hours of skilled labor annually.

Unstructured Data, Inconsistent Formats

The same clinical finding appears as a structured field at one hospital and as free-text in a note at another. Rules-based systems and keyword NLP can't handle this variability reliably.

Patient Voices Go Uncaptured

Patient-reported outcomes are critical for research and value-based care, but most collection methods are fragmented, paper-based, or bolted onto workflows that clinicians won't use.

Where We Apply AI in Healthcare

01

Registry Data Abstraction

Automated extraction for clinical registries (STS, ACC, ACS NSQIP, NCDR). AI that reasons across documentation the way a trained clinical abstractor does—reconciling conflicting notes, interpreting temporal relationships, and applying clinical judgment.

02

Cohort Identification & Trial Readiness

AI-powered screening of EHR data against eligibility criteria. Identify eligible patients across unstructured notes, lab values, medication histories, and imaging results—accelerating clinical trial enrollment.

03

Clinical Documentation Improvement

NLP-driven analysis of physician documentation for completeness, specificity, and coding accuracy. Identify underdocumented conditions, missing severity indicators, and opportunities to improve CDI and HCC capture.

Quality Measures & eCQM Reporting

Population Health Analytics

Build longitudinal patient cohorts from disparate data sources. Risk stratification models, social determinants of health extraction, and predictive analytics for chronic disease management and care coordination.
