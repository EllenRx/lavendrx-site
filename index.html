What We Do

We help research institutions, health systems, and life sciences organizations extract structured insights from clinical data using AI. Our work sits at the intersection of clinical domain expertise and modern language models.

Services

  • EMR Data Extraction Transform unstructured clinical text — physician notes, pathology reports, discharge summaries — into structured, research-ready datasets. Entity recognition, assertion status, relation extraction, and code mapping across major EHR systems.
  • Chart Review Automation Automate retrospective chart abstraction at scale. Extract diagnoses, treatment timelines, adverse events, lab values, and clinical endpoints from free-text documentation while maintaining research-grade accuracy.
  • Patient-Reported Outcomes Design and deploy digital ePRO collection using validated instruments. Automated patient engagement, adaptive survey logic, longitudinal tracking, and EHR integration.
  • Real-World Evidence Regulatory-grade RWE from EHR, claims, and registry data. De-identified data pipelines with full audit trails for observational studies, post-market surveillance, and value-based care reporting.
  • Data Engineering and Compliance HIPAA-compliant data infrastructure. ETL pipelines, clinical data warehousing, automated PHI de-identification. Full audit-ready data lineage for IRB and regulatory review.
  • AI Strategy Assess organizational readiness, identify high-impact use cases, and develop phased implementation roadmaps for responsible AI adoption in clinical and research settings.

Use Cases

  • Registry Data Abstraction Automated extraction for clinical registries. AI that reasons across documentation the way a trained abstractor does — reconciling conflicting notes, interpreting temporal relationships, applying clinical judgment.
  • Cohort Identification AI-powered screening of EHR data against eligibility criteria. Identify eligible patients across unstructured notes, lab values, medication histories, and imaging results.
  • Clinical Documentation Improvement NLP-driven analysis of physician documentation for completeness, specificity, and coding accuracy.
  • Quality Measures Automate extraction of quality measure data from clinical records. Map documentation to eCQM specifications and generate submission-ready datasets.
  • Prior Authorization Intelligence Decode insurance denial patterns, generate evidence-based appeal documentation, and identify prior authorization bottlenecks across payer-drug-diagnosis combinations.

Who We Work With

Academic medical centers, research institutions, health systems, specialty practices, pharmaceutical and biotech companies, clinical research organizations, payers, and digital health companies.

Approach

Discovery and assessment. Solution design. Build and validate against clinician-reviewed ground truth. Deploy within your infrastructure or a compliant managed environment. We report precision, recall, and inter-annotator agreement. No black boxes.
