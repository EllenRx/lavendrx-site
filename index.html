Clinical NLP & Data Extraction

Transform unstructured clinical text—physician notes, pathology reports, discharge summaries—into structured, research-ready datasets using advanced NLP and large language models. Purpose-built for medical entity recognition, clinical coding, and longitudinal data capture.

🔍

Chart Review Automation

Automate retrospective chart abstraction at scale. Our AI systems extract diagnoses, treatment timelines, adverse events, and clinical endpoints from free-text documentation—reducing weeks of manual review to hours while maintaining research-grade accuracy.

📊

Patient-Reported Outcomes

Design and deploy digital ePRO collection platforms using validated instruments (PROMIS, SF-36, disease-specific PROMs). Automated patient engagement, adaptive survey logic, longitudinal tracking, and integration with existing EHR systems.

🧬

Real-World Evidence

Generate regulatory-grade real-world evidence from EHR, claims, and registry data. We build de-identified data pipelines with full audit trails for observational studies, post-market surveillance, and value-based care reporting.

AI Strategy & Roadmapping

Data Engineering & Compliance
